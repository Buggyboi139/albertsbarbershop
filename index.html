Albert's Barber Shop

Located in the heart of Southside Des Moines

Albert’s Barber Shop has been a staple in the community for over 15 years. We specialize in classic gentlemen's haircuts, providing a professional and friendly atmosphere. Our skilled barbers offer precision cuts, fades, and trims tailored to your style. Whether you're looking for a fresh new look or just a regular trim, Albert’s Barbershop is the place to be.

Walk-ins are always welcome, and we pride ourselves on creating a relaxing, old-school experience with a modern touch. Visit us today and leave feeling your best!

Service for All Ages

We pride ourselves on making everyone from kids to seniors feel comfortable in our antique barber chairs. Our experienced staff can cut and style hair for all ages.
