About Albert

Albert graduated from barber school in 1981 and has proudly owned and operated Albert’s Barber Shop for over 15 years. As the shop's sole barber, Albert provides a highly personalized experience, specializing in classic gentlemen's haircuts in a professional and friendly atmosphere. With decades of expertise, he offers precision cuts, fades, and trims tailored to your exact style.

Walk-ins are always welcome. Come enjoy a relaxing, old-school barbering experience in a clean and modern shop. Visit us today and leave feeling your best!
