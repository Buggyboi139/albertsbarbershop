Albert's Barber Shop

Serving the Southside Des Moines Community

With over 15 years of experience, Albert's Barber Shop is a trusted name in the Southside Des Moines area. We specialize in high-quality, traditional haircuts in a friendly and professional setting. Whether you need a precise fade or a simple trim, Albert provides expert styling tailored to your personal preference.

We love walk-ins and aim to offer a relaxing, old-fashioned experience with a modern twist. Come visit us and walk out feeling confident!

Great Hair for Every Generation

Our shop is a comfortable space for everyone, from children to seniors. Our talented barbers are skilled at working with all ages, ensuring a great experience for the whole family.
